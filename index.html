Products and Services

Nagad combines mobile financial and other associated services; it has a diversified product portfolio to cater to all customer segments. Nagad ensures ease of usage through features like Cash In, Cash Out, Send Money, and Mobile Recharge.

Cash In

Cash in service lets you deposit money into your account through Nagad Uddokta.

To deposit money into your Nagad Account:


  • Go to any Nagad Uddokta
  • Let the Uddokta know the amount you want to Cash In
  • Write down your Nagad Account Number and the Cash In amount in the Uddokta Register
  • Pay the amount of money you want to Cash In
  • In exchange, the Uddokta will send balance to your Nagad Account.
  • Cash In done!

Customer and the Uddokta will both get a confirmation message from Nagad. Remember to sign the Uddokta Register before leaving the counter.

Cash Out

Cash out service enables you to withdraw money from your Nagad account.

To Cash Out from Customer’s Nagad Account:


  • Go to any Nagad Uddokta
  • Let the Uddokta know the amount Customer want to Cash Out
  • Write down Customer’s Nagad Account Number and the amount in the Uddokta Register
  • Dial *167# on your mobile for the Nagad Mobile Menu
  • Choose “Cash Out”
  • Choose “From Uddokta”
  • Enter Uddokta’s Nagad Account Number (ask the Uddokta)
  • Enter the amount
  • Enter Customer’s Nagad Mobile Menu PIN
  • Cash out done!
Send Money

Send money service allows you to transfer money from your Nagad account to another Nagad account.

To send money:

  • Go to Customer’s Nagad Mobile Menu by dialing *167#
  • Choose “Send Money”
  • Enter the Nagad Account Number Customer want to send money to
  • Enter the amount Customer want to send
  • Enter a reference about the transaction. (Do not use more than one word, avoid space or special characters)
  • Now enter Customer’s Nagad Mobile Menu PIN to confirm the transaction

Done! Customer and the Receiver both will receive a confirmation message from Nagad

Recharge

Mobile Recharge allows you to recharge mobile airtime through your Nagad account.

Follow the steps below to recharge your own mobile phone or for someone else's mobile phone:

  • Go to your Nagad Mobile Menu by dialing *167#
  • Choose “Mobile recharge”
  • Choose operator
  • Choose connection type
  • Enter the 11-digit mobile number Customer want to buy airtime for
  • Enter the amount of airtime Customer want to buy
  • Enter Customer’s Nagad Mobile Menu PIN to confirm

Done! Customer will receive a confirmation message from Nagad.
