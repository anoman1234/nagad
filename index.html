About us

‘Nagad’ is a brand owned by Third Wave Technologies Limited (TWTL), which is working in the arena of Digital Financial Services. TWTL is a Private Limited Company incorporated under Bangladesh Companies Act 1994.

TWTL has been established by visionary entrepreneurs, who are affiliated with other prominent business houses of Bangladesh.

TWTL is working with Bangladesh Post Office (BPO), the national postal service authority of the country, to digitize its services and ensuring maximum financial inclusion of the underserved population.
